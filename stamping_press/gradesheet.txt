Stamping Press Rubric
10	Program compiles successfully.

10	Program runs correctly as specified in the requirements

10	Any memory allocated with new is released with delete.

10	overflow function is overridden in class derived from streambuf.

10	Plate is (directly or indirectly) inserted on stream creation. Plate is (directly or indirectly) ejected on stream destruction.

10	A space appears on the plate whenever an invalid character is sent to the output stream.

10	Any output that would run beyond the end of the plate is ignored

10	The manipulators row(n) and endrow will flush the buffer and change the row and column as per the requirements.

10	The buffer is properly flushed upon an overflow.

10	A class derived from ostream initializes ios/ostream's buffer pointer with an object derived from the streambuf class.
